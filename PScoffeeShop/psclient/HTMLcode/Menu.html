    Flavorings.

    We will offer a variety of ethically sourced coffee syrups and sauces from the brand DaVinci Gourmet

Cups

We will offer sizes small, medium, and large in all hot beverages. Beverages sizes will include, 20oz, 16oz, 12oz and a 4oz espresso cup. The hot cups will be black, with a pink sleeve. We will offer sizes small, medium, and large in all iced beverages. Beverages sizes will include, 24oz, 20oz and 16oz. The iced cups will be made of a compostable clear plastic.

Straws

Our cafe will offer pink compostable straws or for an extra dollar we will offer a heart shaped reusable straw. Throughout the year as seasons change and holidays approach we will offer a variety of seasonal straws for an extra festive touch.
